Engines of Fury is a revolutionary free-to-play top-down extraction shooter set in a stark, chilling post-apocalyptic world. As a player, you step into the shoes of a survivor, battling for dominance in a hostile environment filled with mutants and other players. The game blends PvPvE action with deep customization, crafting, and survival mechanics, providing a thrilling and immersive experience for all types of gamers.

Engines of Fury was launched with a vision to pioneer a gaming evolution with a free-to-play blockchain game of unparalleled quality, with the goal of becoming THE GAME to introduce & onboard a huge flow of users from the 3.3 billion Web 2 gamers worldwide.

The World of Engines of Fury

Engines of Fury is set in a dystopian future where the world as we know it has ended, but humanity's struggle for survival continues. The game offers a rich narrative backdrop that enhances the player's immersion, making every raid, fight, and escape a significant event in the story of the apocalypse.

Key Features

  • Classless Champion System One of the standout features of Engines of Fury is its classless champion system. Players can perfect their builds through a variety of gear, abilities, and talents, allowing for unique and personalized playstyles. This flexibility ensures that no two champions are the same, providing endless possibilities for strategic gameplay.
  • Intense PvPvE Combat Engines of Fury features four distinct areas where players can engage in intense PvPvE combat. Whether raiding mutants or fighting other players, the goal is always to escape with valuable loot. The extraction phase is particularly thrilling, as players must outwit each other to retain their hard-earned spoils.
  • Crafting and Upgrading Survival in Engines of Fury depends heavily on crafting and upgrading. Players gather loot from their raids and use it to build and enhance their hideouts, craft powerful weapons, and upgrade their gear. This crafting system adds a layer of depth to the game, encouraging players to continuously improve their equipment and strategies.
  • Cooperative Play While survival can be a solitary endeavor, Engines of Fury also offers the option for cooperative play. Players can join forces with friends online for high-stakes adventures with richer rewards. The game’s guild system further enhances the collaborative experience, allowing players to pool resources, construct guild halls, and dominate the world together.
